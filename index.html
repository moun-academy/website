That's exactly who this is for. Many of our members can deliver presentations just fine — it's the unscripted moments that trip them up. The Speaker's Gym specifically trains conversational fluency, not just stage presence.

About 10-15 minutes of daily practice through the Speaker's Gym app, plus one live workshop per week. The system is designed for working professionals with full schedules.
